Our team

Our awesome Rainydays team calls Norway home, surrounded by stunning landscapes. We are men and woman who just love the outdoors, our skilled team puts their outdoor adventures into making premium rain jackets.

We also have our ever-enthusiastic Rainydays dog, Advendure, who brings joy and spontaneity to our team. Even when miles apart, our vibrant team ensures that Rainydays embodies the spirit of adventure in every decision.
